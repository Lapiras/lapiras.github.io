✅ Your mod package is downloading!

Follow these steps to finish building and installing your mod. You only need to do steps 1–2 once.

1
Install CSDK 12 if you haven't already.
Download it from deadlockmodding.pages.dev and unzip it anywhere on your computer.
2
Extract the ZIP you just downloaded anywhere — your Desktop is fine.
3
Double-click build.bat inside the extracted folder.
The first time, it will ask you where CSDK 12 is installed. After that it remembers. It will automatically copy your files and launch CSDK 12.
4
In CSDK 12, find your sound file in the Asset Browser, right-click it, and select Recompile → Full.
5
Pack the addon using the Workshop Manager (top bar of Asset Browser → New → Submit). Your .vpk file will appear in game/citadel_addons/.
6
Copy the .vpk to Deadlock/game/citadel/addons/ and rename it pak01_dir.vpk. Launch the game and enjoy your mod!
⚠️ Windows may show a security warning when running build.bat. Click "More info" → "Run anyway" — this is normal for unsigned scripts.
